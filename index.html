A Pedometer App for Parents to Keep Healthy Life

This app is aimed at parents who enjoy walking.

Imagine that after a long walk with a pedometer, they can input the data into this app, and it will show the data analysis for the data.

what's more, father and mother can also compete with each other to see who walks more :).
